# BetterTeam Shop Extension


Transform your server with a fully-featured team shop system!
Buy and sell items from a beautifully organized GUI with team balance integration!

## Features
* **Multiple Shop Categories** - 7 pre-configured shops: Blocks, Ores, Redstone, Food, Spawners, Mob Drops & MythicMobs items
* **Team Balance Integration** - Purchases use your BetterTeams balance automatically
* **Buy & Sell System** - Sell items back to the shop for money (configurable per-item)
* **Quantity Selector** - Choose exactly how many items you want to buy
* **WildStacker Support** - Buy spawners with stack amounts included
* **MythicMobs Integration** - Sell custom MythicMobs items directly in shop
* **Fully Configurable** - Every item, price, and message can be customized
* **Beautiful GUI** - Clean, intuitive inventory interface with color-coded categories
* **Hot-Reload Support** - Edit shop files and reload without restarting
